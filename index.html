About Me

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. candidate in the Data and Knowledge Research Group (DKR) , School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales (UNSW). I am under the joint supervision of Dr. Xiaoyang Wang and Prof. Wenjie Zhang . My research interests include Graph Reasoning and Few-shot Learning.

Prior to that, I obtained my Master's degree from University of Southampton (UoS) in 2023, supervised by Prof. Timothy J Norman . Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree from Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU) in 2020.
